## What is Acorn?  

Acorn is a macOS image editor designed to combine simplicity with powerful features for both beginners and creative professionals. It offers a **full set of tools for photo editing, graphic design, and digital illustration**, all within a clean and intuitive interface.  

Users can **edit layers, apply filters, and manipulate text and shapes** with ease, making Acorn suitable for a wide range of projects from quick photo touch-ups to complex graphic designs. Its **non-destructive workflow** ensures that original images remain intact, while adjustments and effects can be fine-tuned endlessly.  

The app includes **vector drawing capabilities, shape libraries, and customizable text tools**, allowing for creative freedom when designing logos, banners, or digital artwork. With **real-time preview, smart layers, and multiple blending options**, Acorn streamlines the creative process and enhances productivity.  

Acorn is fully compatible with **Apple Silicon and Intel Macs**, providing fast performance even with high-resolution images or multi-layered compositions. The software supports **popular formats including PNG, JPEG, TIFF, and PSD**, making it easy to collaborate with other tools and workflows.  

Whether improving photos, crafting digital art, or creating web graphics, Acorn provides a **professional editing experience on macOS**, combining efficiency, flexibility, and high-quality output.
